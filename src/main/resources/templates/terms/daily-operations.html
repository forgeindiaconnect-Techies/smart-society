Daily Operations Policy

Rules for resident onboarding, visitor approvals, complaint assignment, and amenity booking workflows.

Resident onboarding Visitor approvals Complaint assignment Amenity booking
Page 1

Resident Onboarding Terms

Residents may be invited by the society admin or approved after verification. The admin must confirm flat ownership, tenancy, and contact details before granting access.

Residents are responsible for keeping profile, family member, vehicle, and contact information accurate inside the platform.

Access may be revoked when a resident vacates, ownership changes, or the admin detects misuse.

Page 2

Visitor Approval Terms

Visitor entries must be created with correct visitor name, purpose, expected time, flat number, and approval status.

Security staff may verify visitor identity at the gate and record check-in/check-out times. Residents are responsible for approving known visitors.

Emergency access, delivery access, and service staff access may follow society-specific rules configured by the admin.

Page 3

Complaint Assignment Terms

Complaints must include clear issue details, category, priority, location, and supporting images when required.

Admins may assign complaints to maintenance teams, vendors, or committee members. Assigned users must update progress honestly and close only after resolution.

Misuse of complaint features, abusive content, or false emergency reporting may lead to account restriction.

Page 4

Amenity Booking Terms

Amenity bookings depend on availability, society rules, time slots, payment requirements, and admin approval settings.

Residents must follow usage rules for clubhouse, gym, guest rooms, parking, party hall, and sports facilities.

Cancellation, refund, damage charges, and guest limits may vary by society policy.
