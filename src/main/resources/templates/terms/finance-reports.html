Finance & Reports Policy

Rules for maintenance billing, online payments, expense approvals, reports, and analytics.

Maintenance billing Online payments Expense approvals Reports and analytics
Page 1

Maintenance Billing Terms

Society admins are responsible for configuring billing cycles, due dates, maintenance amounts, late fees, taxes, and flat-wise charge rules.

Generated bills should be reviewed before publishing to residents. Corrections must be issued through adjustments or revised invoices.

Residents must pay dues according to the society-approved schedule and retain receipts for reference.

Page 2

Online Payment Terms

Online payments may be processed through third-party payment gateways. Gateway fees, settlement timelines, failed transactions, and refunds follow gateway rules.

A payment is treated as successful only after confirmation is received from the payment provider and recorded in SmartSociety.

Residents should not share payment credentials, OTPs, card details, or banking passwords with society staff or platform support.

Page 3

Expense Approval Terms

Expenses must include vendor name, amount, category, invoice date, proof document, and approval status.

Committee members or authorized admins are responsible for approving, rejecting, or requesting clarification for submitted expenses.

SmartSociety records approval history for transparency and audit reference.

Page 4

Reports and Analytics Terms

Reports are generated from data entered by society admins, residents, security staff, maintenance teams, and payment systems.

Analytics should be used for operational decisions, audit support, billing follow-up, visitor review, and complaint tracking.

Exported reports may contain sensitive resident or financial data and must be shared only with authorized users.
