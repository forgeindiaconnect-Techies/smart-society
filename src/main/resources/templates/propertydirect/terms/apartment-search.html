Buy Apartment Terms

PropertyDirect displays owner-listed apartments for sale based on information provided by owners, admins, or authorized representatives. Buyers must verify ownership documents, approvals, encumbrance details, tax receipts, and association dues before making any payment.

Prices, availability, photos, possession status, and amenities may change. PropertyDirect is a listing and workflow platform and does not guarantee legal title or transaction completion.

Page 2

Rent Apartment Terms

Rental listings are intended to connect tenants with owners or authorized agents. Tenants should verify rent, deposit, maintenance charges, lock-in period, notice period, furnishing status, and society rules before agreement signing.

Move-in dates, visitor rules, parking access, pet rules, and utility arrangements are controlled by the owner or society.

Page 3

Premium Apartment Terms

Premium listings may include furnished homes, gated society units, high-value rentals, or assisted search support. Premium visibility does not mean PropertyDirect has independently certified the property.

Users should inspect the property and confirm all promises in writing before paying token amounts or booking fees.

Page 4

BHK Filter Terms

BHK filters help users narrow listings by bedroom count. The actual usable layout, carpet area, balcony count, servant room, study room, or converted spaces must be verified during inspection.

Incorrectly tagged listings may be corrected, hidden, or reviewed by platform admins.

Page 5

Furnished Filter Terms

Furnishing status may mean fully furnished, semi-furnished, or owner-specific included items. Users should confirm exact furniture, appliances, warranty, and replacement responsibilities before finalizing.

Images may not represent the latest condition after previous tenant usage or owner changes.

Page 6

Pet Friendly Filter Terms

Pet-friendly tags depend on owner approval and society rules. Breed restrictions, common-area rules, cleaning duties, noise complaints, and deposits may apply.

Residents must comply with local laws and apartment association policies for pets.

Page 7

Popular Locality Terms

Popular locality links are for browsing convenience. Commute time, school access, metro distance, flooding risk, road conditions, and neighborhood quality must be verified independently.

Locality names may include nearby areas, landmarks, or apartment clusters for search convenience.
