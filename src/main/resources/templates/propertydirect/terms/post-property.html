Post Property Policy

Rules for owners, admins, and authorized representatives posting apartments for rent or sale.

Page 1

Owner Listing Terms

The person posting a property confirms that they are the owner or have authorization from the owner to list the apartment. Unauthorized listings, duplicate postings, fake owner claims, or misleading broker-style listings may be removed.

PropertyDirect may ask for supporting details to confirm ownership or listing permission.

Page 2

Property Detail Terms

Posters must provide accurate apartment type, block, floor, area, furnishing, parking, amenities, possession date, and society details. Misleading details can cause listing suspension.

Material facts such as pending litigation, major repairs, water issues, or association restrictions should be disclosed to interested users.

Page 3

Photos and Media Terms

Uploaded images should represent the actual apartment and must not include unrelated stock photos, offensive content, or private information of current residents.

PropertyDirect may resize, crop, or moderate media for layout, safety, and listing quality.

Page 4

Pricing and Availability Terms

Rent, sale price, deposit, maintenance, token amount, and availability must be kept up to date. Owners are responsible for updating the listing when the apartment is booked, rented, sold, or unavailable.

Negotiations, payment terms, and agreement conditions are between the owner and customer.

Page 5

Verification and Contact Terms

Owner contact details may be shared with interested users according to platform workflow. Posters must not misuse customer data received through PropertyDirect.

Spam, harassment, repeated unsolicited calls, or asking users to pay outside agreed terms may lead to restriction.
