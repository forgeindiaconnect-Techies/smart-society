Plans & Dashboards Policy

Rules for assisted plans, matching alerts, owner contact support, and role dashboards.

Page 1

Assisted Plan Terms

Assisted plans provide guidance, follow-up, and matching help. They do not guarantee final property selection, owner acceptance, agreement execution, or price negotiation outcome.

Page 2

Property Support Terms

Support may include shortlist help, visit coordination, call reminders, and requirement collection. Users must verify property details personally before decisions.

Page 3

Matching Alert Terms

Matching alerts are based on city, locality, budget, property type, and selected filters. Alerts may include approximate matches when exact listings are unavailable.

Page 4

Owner Contact Assistance Terms

Owner contact information is shared for genuine property discussions only. Users must not spam, harass, scrape, resell, or misuse contact information.

Page 5

Super Admin Dashboard Terms

Super Admin access is for platform-level management, including users, plans, listings, reports, and system configuration. Credentials must be protected and not shared.

Page 6

Admin Dashboard Terms

Admins manage property listings, owner requests, verification workflow, lead handling, and local operations. Admin actions should be accurate and auditable.

Page 7

Customer Dashboard Terms

Customers can shortlist apartments, request owner contact, track visits, and manage service/payment requests. Customer data must be accurate and used only for legitimate property needs.
